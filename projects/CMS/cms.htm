CAD Mesh Segementation

 

In this project, a mesh segmentation method based on clustering is developed, dedicated to the CAD mesh model. The triangles of CAD mesh is first clustered into the sparse and the dense regions. Then, different segmentation methods are apply to these two regions and the final result is obtained. This project is developed using QT and OpenSceneGraphics (OSG).
